Art Portfolio

Here is a sample of some of my artwork. I have been drawing and improving on my art since the first grade, where I started out making comics with my friends. Since then, I have taken art classes all throughout grade school and for 4 semesters of high school. I have also worked with ArtWorks Cincinnati on the mural "The Roots of Walnut Hills" as an apprentice. I have received multiple awards and been in multiple art shows as well!

All images here are of my creation, photographing, and ownership. If these images are to be reposted in any way shape or form, contact me.
