Experiences

August 2020 - May 2024: Setion High School INTERalliance

My experiences with the Seton High School INTERalliance started freshman year when the club was struggling due to Covid-19 restrictions on social activities. I persisted with the club throughout its low peak in participation during my Sophomore year, and for my Junior year, some seniors and I took to reviving the club. With me as the secretary, we participated in chapter competitions, winning second place in our first one of web design. We also attended the 2023 TechOlympics as a chapter. For my Senior year, I was nominated and unaminously elected president. We participated in four total competitions including the 2024 TechOlympics. In all, we placed first for our prototype ADHD-assistance app "ADH-Free" and second in our educational video game "Cat Math" (link pending). For our efforts, we won Chapter of the Year and were the chapter with the most participation.

August 2022 - May 2023: University of Cincinnati CCP Engineering

I took and passed both ENED 1100 and ENED 1120 as a junior in highschool, earning college credit and contributing to my college GPA. Skills from the course included LEGO robotics, Matlab, Excel, Python, Spatial Visulization, math with Related Rates, Statistics, and more. I completed the courses with an A in each.
